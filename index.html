About Me

Hello! My name is Katherine Wong; I'm an undergraduate student at Stanford University studying Symbolic Systems (Computer Science + Psychology/Philosophy) with double-minors in Creative Writing and Data Science. I've been tutoring students in writing for over two years now and am very passionate about exposing students to new storytelling tools and diverse works of writing. In the past, my work has been recognized by the Kenyon Review, Vermont College of Fine Arts, and the Blue Marble Review; I also hold editing experience with Stanford's Leland Quarterly Literary Magazine and Inkblot Magazine. Feel free to reach out if you have any questions!
